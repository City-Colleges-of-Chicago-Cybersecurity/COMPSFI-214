# Spring 2026
**City Colleges of Chicago** 

This is a fast-paced course designed to reflect the rigor and scope of real-world cybersecurity practice. The CISSP framework used in this course represents one of the most demanding and comprehensive certifications in the field, requiring consistent effort, critical thinking, and disciplined study habits. As a result, course material will move quickly, and students are expected to stay engaged and manage their time effectively. There will be few opportunities to make up missed work, and procrastination will significantly impact performance. Success in this course depends on steady progress, active participation, and meeting deadlines as scheduled.
